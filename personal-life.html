The Voracious Reader

Jayalalithaa maintained a private library of over 8,000 books at her Poes Garden residence, reading late into the night even during intense political campaigns.

Classical Dance Maestro

Trained in Bharatanatyam, Kathak, Mohiniyattam, and Manipuri, her performances captivated audiences and reflected the deep artistic soul within the political leader.

Art & Cultural Heritage

Her love for classical arts extended beyond dance — she was a trained Carnatic singer and collected rare traditional Indian paintings and vintage stamps.

PRIVATE SPHERE

Family & Personal Life

Family & Companions

Father

Jayaram

A lawyer by training who hailed from a wealthy family in Mysore. He passed away in 1950 when Jayalalithaa was only two years old.

Mother

Vedavalli (Sandhya)

A strong-willed woman who worked as a clerk before entering cinema under the screen name 'Sandhya' to support her children. She was Jayalalithaa's primary source of strength and guidance.

Brother

Jayakumar

Jayalalithaa's elder brother, with whom she shared a close childhood. He resided in Chennai and passed away in 1995.

Political Mentor

M.G. Ramachandran (MGR)

A legendary actor and former Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu. He introduced Jayalalithaa to politics, serving as her mentor and guide throughout her early political career.

Lifelong Companion

V.K. Sasikala

A close confidante who lived with Jayalalithaa at her Poes Garden residence, Veda Nilayam. She managed household affairs and stood by her through major political and legal challenges.

Personal Interests

  • Voracious reading - maintained a private library of over 8,000 rare books.
  • Classical Carnatic music - trained in singing and performed classical pieces.
  • Classical Dance - proficient in Bharatanatyam, Kathak, and Mohiniyattam.
  • Horticulture & Gardening - loved spending time in tea estates and gardening.

Hobbies & Pastimes

  • Writing articles, columns, and letters in English and Tamil.
  • Listening to old Tamil and English classics.
  • Collecting rare books, stamps, and traditional Indian paintings.

Academic Top Scorer

Reveal Fact
Historical Fact

She secured the first rank in the whole state of Tamil Nadu in her Matriculation exams. She was awarded a gold medal by the government but had to skip college for films due to family debt.

Oscar Connection

Record-Breaking Salary

Historical Fact
